About Us

Includables is a fintech startup enabling African families around the world to create, manage, grow and preserve generational wealth.

Our flagship product is a one-stop money app that gives tweens, teens and adults the privacy and security to manage their personal finance and learn financial literacy all in one place as well as the choice to collaborate on money goals with their family and friends.
